FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while complex web applications can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What technologies do you work with?

+

We work with modern technologies including React, Next.js, Node.js, PHP, Python, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and cloud platforms like AWS and Google Cloud. Our tech stack is always evolving to stay current.

Do you provide ongoing support?

+

Yes! We offer various support packages including bug fixes, updates, security patches, and feature enhancements. All projects include a 30-day warranty period.

What is your pricing structure?

+

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects, hourly rates, and retainer agreements. Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and timeline requirements.

Can you work with our existing team?

+

Absolutely! We frequently collaborate with in-house teams and can provide additional expertise, code reviews, or take on specific components of larger projects.
